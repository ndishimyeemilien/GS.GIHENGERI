Our Goals for the Year

About Us

At College Baptiste Gacuba II, we strive for academic excellence, fostering the growth of young minds in an inclusive and supportive environment.

Our mission is to provide a well-rounded education, promoting both intellectual and personal development to shape future leaders.

Our Goals for This Year

Academic Excellence

We will continue to improve teaching methods and ensure that our students achieve the highest academic standards.

Holistic Growth

Fostering both intellectual and personal growth through a variety of extracurricular activities and values-based education.

Community Engagement

Building strong relationships with our local community and strengthening the bond between students, teachers, and families.

Environmental Responsibility

Implementing sustainable practices and educating students on the importance of caring for the environment.

Obligations and Expectations for Students

  • Academic Integrity: Uphold high standards of honesty and academic excellence.
  • Respect: Treat all members of the school community with kindness and respect.
  • Participation: Engage actively in academic and extracurricular activities.
  • Responsibility: Be punctual, complete assignments on time, and contribute positively to the school environment.
