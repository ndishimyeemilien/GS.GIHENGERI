School Visiting Day Promotes Collaboration

On August 20th, GS.gihenger organized a visiting day for students to explore educational opportunities at other schools. The initiative aims to broaden students’ horizons, encourage collaboration, and exchange knowledge between institutions.

During this event, students had the chance to tour the other school’s facilities, meet their peers, and attend informative workshops. The goal of the visiting day is not only to inspire students but also to help them gain a deeper understanding of different educational systems.

The event was highly praised by both teachers and students. Many expressed that it was a valuable experience, one that will encourage them to explore new learning methods and build friendships with students from other schools.

Photo: Students from College Baptiste Gacuba II visiting University Of Tourism

Highlights of the Day

  • Interactive workshops on various subjects
  • Group discussions between students from different schools
  • Student performances showcasing talent and creativity
  • A tour of the host school’s campus and amenities

We look forward to organizing more such events in the future, building stronger ties between our schools, and fostering a culture of mutual respect and academic excellence.
